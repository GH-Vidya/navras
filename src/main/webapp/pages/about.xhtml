I am Vyjayanthi Iyer, a model, actress, dancer and choreographer from Mumbai, India. I have been a resident of Amsterdam for 10 years, and enjoyed every bit of the multicultural, vibrant, free-spirited life it offers. I have also worked as a Customer Relationship management professional for over 10 years and experienced rich interactions with people from different cultures and countries.

I have been always passionate about teaching dance. My passion for teaching dance grew when I saw people absolutely love to do Bollywood dance. Bollywood dance has a combination of classical, semi classical, western dance moves which are getting popular within the Bollywood numbers . I started my Bollywood dance school called NAVRAS- 9 emotions 6 years ago. In the last 6 years, my passion for dance has expanded into conducting workshops for smaller and bigger groups and companies, fitness classes (Bollywzumba) and for special occasions like bachelor parties etc

Bollywood dancing is lively, it is fun, exciting and the one thing I have never failed to notice is the smile it brings to the faces of people who participate in my workshops. I look forward to taking this endeavour further to exchanging knowledge about Indian culture and take Bollywood dance moves to more people in the Netherlands and beyond.
